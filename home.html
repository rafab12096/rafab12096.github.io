We aim to provide bright and underpriviledged students in Brazil with access to state of the art private school education, enabling them to build a strong foundation for long lasting impact and leadership potential. Our objective is to build a powerful conduit towards social mobility, one of the key challenges of Brazil. Through our donors' support, we are able to change the lives of hard working and highly driven students who would not have otherwise the means to access the best schools in their communities. In addition to private school placement, our program also offers scholarships to second language programs, enabling our students to develop the necessary skills to become a well rounded student with no geographic boundaries.
